Our Progress

The aim project is to help animals who have simply found themselves without a home due to life circumstances. We help them to undergo treatment and rehabilitation and to find a new family. We also want to draw public attention to the problems of such animals and unite professional communities from differebt countries to solve existing problems.

We are sure there are no boundaries and obstacles to achieve the project's goal! By combining our efforts we can do it!
